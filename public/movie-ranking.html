The Best K Dramas According to Shey

My K Drama Must Watch List

Reborn Rich

Living in his new life, the man tries to get revenge upon the family that once killed him. The faithful servant of a chaebol family dies and then reincarnates in the 1980s as a son and heir of their family. Living in his new life, the man tries to get revenge upon the family that once killed him.

Alchemy of Souls

Set in a fictional country called Daeho, it is about the love and growth of young mages as they overcome their twisted fates due to a forbidden magic spell known as the "alchemy of souls", which allows souls to switch bodies

Crash Landing On You

After a South Korean heiress crash-lands (quite literally) into North Korea after a paragliding accident, she meets a North Korean soldier who helps her escape; romance ensues, obviously. The series has garnered praise from Korean and foreign viewers alike, and has climbed its way into the hearts of many.
